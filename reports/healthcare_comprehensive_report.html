Metrics Validation Framework

To ensure accuracy and transparency in our metrics reporting, we've implemented a comprehensive validation system with the following components:

  • Weighted Overall Score: The system now uses a weighted scoring approach, with 75% of the overall score coming from enhanced metrics (Customer Experience, AI, Machine Learning) and 25% from traditional domain metrics.
  • Consistency Checks: Automated validation ensures metrics are consistent across different reporting components.
  • Metrics Delta Tracking: The system now explicitly tracks improvements from baseline for each metric area.
  • Tamper Prevention: Checksums ensure the integrity of reported metrics.

Metrics Validation Benefits

This new approach ensures that targeted improvements in critical performance areas are properly reflected in the overall system score, providing a more accurate representation of system enhancements.
